Crystal Collector

Rules

Welcome! You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total.

You win the game by matching your total to the magic number displayed. But don't get trigger-happy! Go over the magic number, and you lose!

Each of the crystals have a hidden number that you won't know until you click on it.

Finally, the game resets at a win or loss, and the magic number, as well as the crystals, will change! Good luck!
